A Model of AI-First Private Equity Firm

An Open Source Initiative

SmallPE is an open-source project that explores what an AI-first Private Equity firm could look like. We're reimagining the traditional PE model by harnessing the power of artificial intelligence across all aspects of investment management.

Our mission is to create a collaborative ecosystem where professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts can contribute to building the future of intelligent capital allocation.

Our Philosophy

AI-Powered Decision Making

We believe that integrating artificial intelligence into the investment process can enhance decision-making, reduce bias, and identify opportunities that human analysis alone might miss.

Collaborative Innovation

SmallPE thrives on the collective intelligence of our volunteer community. We foster an environment where financial experts, technologists, and innovators work together to create cutting-edge solutions.

Transparent Methodologies

We're committed to openness in our approach. By making our research, methodologies, and models available to our community, we create opportunities for continuous improvement and knowledge sharing.
