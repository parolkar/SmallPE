# Theory of Change Template

## Overview

Theory of Change is a systematic methodology for articulating how your investment creates measurable positive impact. It maps the logical pathway from inputs through activities, outputs, outcomes, and ultimately to long-term impact.

This template helps you develop rigorous Impact Theses that complement Fund Theses, ensuring impact promises are achievable, measurable, and financially aligned.

## Quality Control Checklist

Before finalizing your Theory of Change, ensure:

**Rigor & Credibility:**
- [ ] Impact pathway is logical and evidence-based
- [ ] Assumptions are explicitly stated and testable
- [ ] Metrics are specific, measurable, and verifiable
- [ ] Attribution method is defensible
- [ ] Baseline data exists or can be established

**Financial Alignment:**
- [ ] Impact goals support (not hinder) financial returns
- [ ] Dual-return opportunities clearly identified
- [ ] Trade-offs acknowledged and addressed
- [ ] Impact initiatives have realistic budgets

**Authenticity & Materiality:**
- [ ] Impact is core to business model, not token
- [ ] SDG alignment is specific and material
- [ ] Claims are defensible and evidence-backed
- [ ] Avoids "impact washing" red flags

**Actionability:**
- [ ] Clear ownership and accountability
- [ ] Monitoring systems are practical
- [ ] Reporting frequency is realistic
- [ ] Corrective action plans defined
