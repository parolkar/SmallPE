## Integration with Deal Process

### Pre-Investment Phase

**Thesis Development (Oracle + Iris):**
- Document all impact promises in fund thesis
- Verify each promise has Theory of Change backing
- Ensure impact thesis aligns with fund thesis

**Deal Sourcing (Helios):**
- Document impact promises in opportunity snapshots
- Flag material impact opportunities for Iris review
- Create preliminary Impact Promise Register entries

### Due Diligence Phase (Athena + Iris)

**Impact Due Diligence Workstream:**
- [ ] Create Impact Promise Register for deal
- [ ] Add impact-specific requests to MRL
- [ ] Validate baseline metrics with Themis
- [ ] Assess feasibility of achieving promises
- [ ] Document impact risks
- [ ] Include Impact Promise summary in IC memo

**MRL Integration:**
- Link Impact Promises to specific MRL requests
- Track evidence gathering for each promise
- Connect to Themis Evidence IDs

### Investment Decision

**IC Review:**
- [ ] Present Impact Promise Register to IC
- [ ] Highlight promises requiring post-close validation
- [ ] Confirm accountability ownership
- [ ] Get approval for impact monitoring plan

### Post-Close (100-Day Plan)

**Impact Activation:**
- [ ] Confirm baselines for all metrics
- [ ] Establish data collection systems
- [ ] Set up quarterly reporting cadence
- [ ] Embed impact KPIs in management dashboards
- [ ] Include impact goals in management incentive plans

### Portfolio Management

**Quarterly Reviews:**
- [ ] Update delivery status for all active promises
- [ ] Identify blockers and corrective actions
- [ ] Report to Board on impact performance
- [ ] Escalate significantly behind promises

**Annual Assessment:**
- [ ] Comprehensive impact performance review
- [ ] Validate continued relevance of promises
- [ ] Adjust targets if business conditions changed materially
- [ ] Report to LPs on impact delivery

### Exit

**Impact Handoff:**
- [ ] Prepare impact performance summary for new owner
- [ ] Document impact systems and processes
- [ ] Extract learnings for next fund thesis
- [ ] Create impact case study if performance strong

## Accountability Framework

### Roles & Responsibilities

**Iris (Impact Investing Strategist)**:
- Owns and maintains Impact Promise Register
- Conducts quarterly reviews of all promises
- Flags at-risk promises to Managing Partner
- Ensures evidence-based impact claims
- Reports impact performance to LPs

**Athena (Due Diligence Orchestrator)**:
- Integrates Impact Promise Register with MRL
- Coordinates impact due diligence
- Ensures promises are validated during diligence

**Themis (Evidence Verifier)**:
- Verifies evidence for all impact promises
- Links Impact Promise IDs to Evidence IDs
- Flags unverified or contradicted promises

**Prometheus (Business Evaluator)**:
- Identifies impact-linked value creation opportunities
- Ensures promises align with business reality
- Documents impact in business evaluations

**Management (Portfolio Company)**:
- Delivers on impact promises
- Reports impact metrics quarterly
- Escalates issues preventing delivery

**SmallPE Board**:
- Reviews impact performance quarterly
- Approves corrective actions for behind promises
- Ensures resources allocated to impact delivery

**Managing Partner**:
- Final accountability for impact delivery
- Annual LP reporting on impact performance
- Strategic decisions on impact-financial trade-offs

### Escalation Process

**Tier 1: Moderate Concern (70-90% of target)**
- Iris flags to portfolio company management
- Discuss in next board meeting
- Document plan to get back on track

**Tier 2: Significant Concern (<70% of target)**
- Iris escalates to Managing Partner
- Board deep dive on root causes
- Formal corrective action plan required
- Monthly monitoring until resolved

**Tier 3: Critical Concern (contradicted or invalidated)**
- Immediate escalation to Managing Partner
- Emergency board meeting if material promise
- Decision: Revise promise, double-down on resources, or withdraw claim
- LP communication if promise was made publicly
